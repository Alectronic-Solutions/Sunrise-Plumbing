Our Services

Do you repair all water heater brands?

Yes. Our Austin plumbers are trained to repair and replace all major water heater brands including Rheem, AO Smith, Bradford White, Navien, Rinnai, State, Whirlpool, and more. We service both tank and tankless water heaters. See our water heater repair service page for details.

How do I know if I need a new water heater or just a repair?

If your water heater is under 8 years old and has a single issue (bad element, thermostat, or valve), repair is usually the better choice. Water heaters over 10-12 years old, or those with tank corrosion, sediment buildup, or multiple failures, typically need replacement. Our Austin plumbers will inspect and give you an honest recommendation. We won't push a replacement if a repair makes sense.

What causes drains to clog, and how do you fix them?

Common causes of drain clogs in Austin homes include grease buildup (kitchen), hair and soap scum (bathroom), tree root intrusion in older clay pipes, and mineral deposits from Austin's hard water. We use professional hydro-jetting and mechanical snake equipment to clear clogs completely, not just punch a hole through them. Clogs cleared with hydro-jetting typically stay clear 3–5x longer than snake-only service. See our drain cleaning service page.

Can you detect water leaks without tearing up my walls?

Yes. Sunrise Plumbing uses non-invasive leak detection technology including acoustic listening devices and thermal imaging cameras to locate leaks behind walls, under slabs, and underground, without unnecessary demolition. We find the leak first, then discuss the least invasive repair option. See our leak detection service page.

What plumbing services do you offer for bathrooms?

Process & What to Expect

What should I do while waiting for an emergency plumber?

If you have a burst pipe or major leak, turn off the main water shutoff valve immediately (usually located near your water meter or under the kitchen sink). For a water heater leak, turn off the cold water supply to the unit. Avoid electrical hazards near standing water. Call us at (512) 555-0100. We'll talk you through any immediate steps while we're on the way.

Do I need to be home for the service visit?

Yes, an adult (18+) should be present during the service visit. We will call or text you 30 minutes before arrival so you can plan accordingly. If you can't be home, contact us to arrange an alternative. We occasionally work with property managers who can provide access.

Do you typically fix the problem in one visit?

Yes. Our vans carry thousands of common parts so the vast majority of repairs are completed in a single visit. Before we come out, we'll ask you to describe the problem so we can stock the most likely parts. In rare cases where a specialty part needs ordering, we'll be upfront about that before starting.
