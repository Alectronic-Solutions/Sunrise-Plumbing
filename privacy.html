Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact Sunrise Plumbing through our website contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide: your name, phone number, email address, service address, and description of your plumbing needs. We do not collect payment information through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your service request, schedule appointments, and provide plumbing services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Contact Form

Our contact form submits your information securely. Form submissions are delivered to our team's email and may be stored in our scheduling system. Submissions are used only for service coordination.

4. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. This data is anonymized and aggregated. We cannot identify individual visitors from analytics data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide. Our website is served over HTTPS. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us at info@sunriseplumbing.com or calling (512) 555-0100.
