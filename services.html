Water Heater Repair & Replacement Austin TX

No hot water in Austin TX? Sunrise Plumbing provides same-day water heater repair and replacement for all brands: Rheem, AO Smith, Bradford White, Navien, Rinnai, and more. We service both traditional tank and modern tankless units.

Austin's hard water accelerates sediment buildup and corrosion. Our licensed plumbers diagnose the problem quickly and give you an honest recommendation: repair if it makes sense, replace only when necessary.

Signs You Need Water Heater Service

  • No hot water or inconsistent temperatures
  • Rusty or discolored water from hot tap
  • Rumbling, popping, or banging sounds from the unit
  • Water pooling around the base of the heater
  • Unit is over 10–12 years old
  • Dramatically increased gas or electric bills

Our Water Heater Services Include

  • Same-day water heater repair in Austin TX
  • Tank water heater replacement (all sizes)
  • Tankless water heater installation and repair
  • Anode rod replacement and sediment flush
  • Pressure relief valve testing and replacement
  • Energy-efficient upgrade consulting

Leak Detection Austin TX: Non-Invasive Technology

A hidden water leak can silently destroy your walls, flooring, and foundation. Sunrise Plumbing provides advanced leak detection in Austin TX using acoustic listening devices and thermal imaging cameras to locate leaks without unnecessary demolition.

Signs of a hidden leak include unexplained increases in your Austin Water bill, the sound of running water when everything is off, warm spots on floors, mold growth, or cracks in your foundation. We find the leak, then fix it.

Types of Leaks We Detect & Repair

  • Slab leaks (under-foundation pipe leaks)
  • Behind-wall pipe leaks
  • Underground supply line leaks
  • Pool and irrigation system leaks
  • Roof and attic plumbing leaks
  • Meter-to-house main line leaks

Suspicious water bill? Call (512) 555-0100. Our Austin leak detection service is fast, non-invasive, and accurate.
