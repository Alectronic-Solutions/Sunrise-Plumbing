Our Story

Built in Austin. Built on Trust.

Sunrise Plumbing was founded in 2009 by Marcus Rivera, a third-generation plumber who grew up in Austin TX and wanted to build a plumbing company the city could actually trust. The name reflects Marcus's belief that every homeowner deserves a clean start after a stressful plumbing problem.

What started as a one-man operation out of a single van has grown into a team of 12 licensed Austin plumbers, covering the entire metro area with the same commitment to honesty and quality Marcus established from day one.

We've never taken outside investment. We're privately owned, debt-free, and accountable only to our customers. That independence means flat-rate pricing, no high-pressure sales tactics, and work we'd stand behind in our own homes.

Our Fleet

Fully Stocked Vans. Fixed Right the First Time.

Every Sunrise Plumbing van carries the parts most likely needed for common Austin plumbing repairs: water heater elements, supply lines, wax rings, shut-off valves, PEX fittings, and more. We fix the problem in a single visit instead of driving back for parts.

Our 8 service vans are GPS-tracked and dispatched by our Austin-based team. When you call at 2am, a real person answers, looks at where our vans are, and sends the closest available plumber to your door.
